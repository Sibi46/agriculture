❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our program

Who can join the program?

Underprivileged rural youth and children interested in organic farming and entrepreneurship. No prior farming experience required.

Is education free?

Yes, education is provided free of cost for eligible students. We focus on making quality farming education accessible to all.

Do students earn money during training?

Yes! Students earn through farm produce selling, e-commerce sales, workshops, and farm tourism activities. Education without debt.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, we provide eco-friendly mud hostels where students live with the land, experiencing simple living and community bonding.

What happens after course completion?

Graduates receive land access support, business network connections, and lifelong alumni community support to start their farming journey.

What skills will students learn?

Organic farming, soil science, seed preservation, business management, digital marketing, communication skills, and entrepreneurship.
