Agriculture School for Organic & Natural Farming - From Soil to Self-Reliance

An agriculture school built for underprivileged children who deserve more than survival they deserve ownership, dignity, and leadership. We teach them to grow food, build enterprises, and rebuild villages.

Not charity. Opportunity

OUR PURPOSE

Education That Feeds the Future

Millions of rural children grow up around farms but never learn how to own one profitably.

We envision to change that.

This is a school where soil becomes strength and farming becomes freedom.

Vision

To build India's first fully practical, income-generating Organic & Natural Farming School that transforms underprivileged youth into profitable farm entrepreneurs and village Organic and natural Farming leaders.

Mission

• Teach 100% practical organic and natural farming

• Integrate farming + business + communication + marketing

• Provide land access post graduation

• Create a tribe-based alumni ecosystem (Farm to consumer)

• Make students income-generating while learning

• Prepare and develop for international opportunities

OUR PROGRAM

Learning With Hands in the Soil

This is not classroom theory. It's real crops, real income, real markets.

Students learn organic and natural farming, farm business, digital selling, and communication all while practicing daily on the land.

ORGANIC LAB & DIGITAL CLASSROOM

Where Science Meets Nature

We teach natural formulations, seed preservation, soil health, and sustainable methods alongside communication and digital skills.

Ancient wisdom. Modern tools. One purpose.

STUDENT FARM PLOTS

Own Piece of Earth

Every student receives a personal farming plot to cultivate. They plan it. They grow it. They sell it.

From Day One, they are farmers not spectators.

ORGANIC & NATURAL E-COMMERCE PORTAL

From Village Soil to Global Market

Students learn how to sell what they grow through our digital organic marketplace. They manage products, pricing, branding, and online sales turning crops into income.

Technology meets tradition.

EARTH-FRIENDLY LIVING

Living With the Land

Our mud-built hostels are simple, breathable, and sustainable. Students live together, eat together, grow together forming bonds that last a lifetime.

Community is our classroom too.

ECO KITCHEN & DINING

Farm to Plate, Every Day

Students cook with the vegetables they grow. Food is not just nutrition it is responsibility and pride.

They taste the result of their own effort.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our program

Who can join the program?

Underprivileged rural youth and children interested in organic farming and entrepreneurship. No prior farming experience required.

Is education free?

Yes, education is provided free of cost for eligible students. We focus on making quality farming education accessible to all.

Do students earn money during training?

Yes! Students earn through farm produce selling, e-commerce sales, workshops, and farm tourism activities.
